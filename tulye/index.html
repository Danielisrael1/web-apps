About TULYE

TULYE Restaurant was founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Tulye with a vision to bring authentic flavors to our community. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most beloved dining establishments in the area.

Our commitment to using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods has earned us numerous accolades and a loyal customer base.
